Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

At Taprobane Lager, operated by Two Cousins Culinary Collections, we collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when expressing an interest in obtaining information about us or our products. This includes your name, email address, phone number, and any other details you provide through our contact forms or checkout process.

2. Age Verification

As a premium beer brand, we are legally required to ensure that our users are of legal drinking age in their respective regions. Our website utilizes an age gate to verify your age before granting access to our content. We do not store this age verification data persistently linking it to your identity, but we do set a local cookie or local storage token to prevent repeated prompts on the same device.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect or receive to facilitate account creation and logon process, to send you marketing and promotional communications, and to fulfill and manage your orders. Your data helps us improve our services and your user experience on our platform.

4. Sharing Your Information

We only share information with your consent, to comply with laws, to provide you with services, to protect your rights, or to fulfill business obligations. We securely process payments through trusted third-party providers (like Stripe and PayPal) and do not directly store your credit card details on our servers.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to access or store information. These are used primarily for the functionality of our shopping cart, language preferences, and age verification to ensure a smooth browsing experience.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this notice, you may email us at taprobanelager@gmail.com or by post to our headquarters in Alberta, Canada.
