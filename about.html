The Heritage

Two Cousins
Culinary Collections

Established in 2018, Two Cousins Culinary Collections embarked on a journey to bring exceptional, culturally inspired flavors to the heart of North America.

Taprobane Lager is the culmination of our passion for brewing and our deep roots in Asian heritage. "Taprobane" is the historical name for Sri Lanka.

We brew strictly in Alberta, utilizing the world-renowned Alberta grains to ensure an unparalleled quality. It is a harmonious marriage of local excellence and global inspiration.

"Brewed in Alberta, Inspired by Asia"

Our philosophy is simple: Create an uncompromisingly premium lager that honors the traditions of our ancestors while celebrating the pristine ingredients of our home in Alberta.
